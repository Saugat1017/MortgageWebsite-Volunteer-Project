Mortgage Tips & Insights

Understanding Your Payment

Your monthly mortgage payment includes principal, interest, property taxes, and insurance (PITI). Understanding this breakdown helps you budget effectively.

Down Payment Impact

A larger down payment reduces your loan amount and monthly payment. It can also help you avoid private mortgage insurance (PMI).

Interest Rate Matters

Even a small difference in interest rate can significantly impact your total cost over the life of the loan. Shop around for the best rates.
