About Landing Solution

Your trusted partner in home financing for over 25 years

Our Mission & Values

After more than 25 years in the finance industry, I am proud to continue serving clients as part of Nexa Mortgage through Don Mario’s Lending Solutions. My mission is to simplify the mortgage process and provide tailored solutions that help individuals and business owners achieve their financial goals. Throughout my career, I have financed a wide range of projects — from residential mortgages and investment properties to commercial buildings and retail stores. My background with conventional financial institutions gave me deep expertise in lending, but it also showed me the limitations many banks face when it comes to product flexibility and client options.

Now, with Don Mario’s Lending Solutions, I have more lending products and tools than ever before to meet the diverse needs of my clients. Whether it’s conventional loans, SBA financing, or investment property lending, I can provide solutions that best fit your financial situation. Because I maintain control of the lending process from start to finish, I can ensure that transactions are efficient, smooth, and timely — with closings in as little as 5 days for unique situations and an average of just 14 days.

At the heart of everything I do is my commitment to putting customers first. My goal is to make the dream of homeownership a reality and to help business owners secure the properties they need to grow and thrive. With dedication, transparency, and speed, I am here to make your lending experience a positive and successful one.
